About us

Established in 2023, DMS Ganadamane is located in the Western Ghats region of Karnataka, Udupi district. We specialise in cultivating and processing premium spices, rice, groundnut, and filter coffee powder, bringing the finest produce directly from nature to your home.

Founded by Divyashri Mahesha Ganiga, who holds a Master's Degree in Economics with expertise in food product trading, marketing, consumer behaviour, and public policy, DMS Ganadamane is built on a foundation of responsible, ethical, and transparent business practices.

We export significant quantities of rice, filter coffee powder, dry red chillies, and other spices to European countries including Germany, France, and the Netherlands. Our head office is in Yelith Village, Udupi, with branches in Bengaluru, Vijayawada, and across Karnataka. Our goal is to continuously innovate, maintain the highest quality standards, and serve our customers in the best possible way.
